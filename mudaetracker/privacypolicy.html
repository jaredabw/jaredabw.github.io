Privacy Policy

For questions or help, join the support server at discord.gg/Wnk5P2krtq

This Bot (Mudae Tracker) does not collect any sensitive data.

The data collected by the Bot includes:
- User snowflake IDs.
- Channel and Guild snowflake IDs.
- Statistics relevant to Mudae, ie. roll count and wish count.
- Personal settings and guild configurations.
- General logs about the Bot's usage; usernames, guild names, guild member counts.

During the usage of the Bot, in addition to above, the Bot processes but does not save:
- Messages sent within the Guild by Mudae, for the purpose of checking rolls and other events.
- Messages sent within the Guild by users, for the purpose of checking matching commands.
- Messages sent to the Bot
- User avatars.
- Data sent by Mudae relating to the Game, such as images.

This website does not collect any data.
